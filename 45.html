AZAAN is SUNNAT only for the five FARZ SALAAH and for JUMUAH SALAAH.
AZAAN is not required for any other Salaah.
AZAAN means to inform, but in Shariat AZAAN means to inform of a particular Salaah in
particular wordings.
The AZAAN for every Farz Salaah should be called out in its prescribed time.
If it was called before the time then it must be called again at the right time.
