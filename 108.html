Rasulullah (S.A.W.) has said: "Mention the GOOD actions off your deceased and abstain from the
OFFENSIVE ones."
The time for TA'ZIAT extends for THREE days after the death. It is MAKROOH to make
TA'ZIAT after this period, except in cases where one is NOT present at the Janaza or when the
bereaved is absent. Ta'ziat before the burial is permissible.
VISITING THE GRAVEYARD (
QABRASTAN)
Rasulullah (S.A.W.) has mentioned: "Visit the graves, for surely visiting the graves lessens
worldly love and reminds you of the hereafter. "
The Qabrastaan could be visited on any day. Friday is preferred to this visit, and if possible it
should be weekly visit. It has been related in a Hadith that : "Whoever will visit his parents'
grave every Friday will be granted Maghfirat and he will be recorded as an obedient son of his
parents.

WHAT TO RECITE WHEN IN THE QABRASTAN.
There are many supplications that may be read at the grave side, the best being the recital of the
Holy Qur’an stand facing the grave (back towards the Qibla) and recite as much of the Qur'an as
possible and make Du’aa for the Maghfirat (forgiveness) of the departed. A few ways of praying
for the dead, as related in the AHAADEES, are given hereafter.
