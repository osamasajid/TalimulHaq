11. SA'EE BETWEEN SARA AND MARWA:
One should not return to the Hajare Aswad and make Istilaam.
Whilc making Istilaam. say :
(Allah is great, there is no diety besides Allah).
This Istilaam is Mustahab.
After this, proceed to Sata, (preferably through Babus Safa) On reaching Safa, climb onto the
rock and facing the Ka'ba, say the Niyyah, (which is Sunnah) :
(O Allah, I intend performing Sa'ee between and Safa and Marwah, seven circuits for Allah,
therefore, make it easy for me, and accept it from me).
