Bismillahi Ar-Rahmani Ar-Rahim
1- Kul huwa Allahu ahad
2- Allahu-s-samad
3- Laialed ualamulad
4- Ualamiaku-l-lahu kufuwan ahad
In the name of Allah, Most Gracious, Most Merciful
1.
Say: He is Allah, the One and Only;
2.
Allah, the Eternal, Absolute;
3.
He begetteth not, nor is He begotten;
4.
And there is none like unto Him.
Surat
Al-Falaq
The Daybreak, Dawn (Quran Surah 113)
Bismillahi Ar-
Rahmani Ar-Rahim
1- Kul A'udhu bi-
Rabbi-l-falaq
2- Minsherri ma-khalaq
3- Wa minsherri ghasiqin idha Waqab
4- Wa minsherri n-naffathati fi-l-'Uqad
5- Wa minsherri hasidin idha hasad
In the name of Allah, Most Gracious, Most Merciful
1.
Say: I seek refuge with the Lord of the Dawn
2.
From the mischief of created things;
3.
From the mischief of Darkness as it overspreads;
4.
From the mischief of those who practice secret arts;
5.
And from the mischief of the envious one as he practices envy.
