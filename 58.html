If there is a valid reason the forehead could be kept off the ground, otherwise the Sajdah will not
be valid. When in Sajdah the feet should not be lifted from the ground. If they are lifted for
duration more than three "SUBHANALLAH" the Salaah will be void.
WOMEN
•
In Sajdah the upper part of the leg (the thigh) should not be upright but in as flat a position as possible,
with the feet spread towards the right.
•
While in Sajdah the stomach and thighs must be kept together.
•
The forearms should be put flat on the ground in Sajdah.
Women must not raise their VOICE when reciting TASBEEH.

JALSA
(To sit between two Sajdahs)
Saying ALLAHU AKBAR sit up straight, do not sit with the back crooked or stooped. It is
important to sit up and pause after the first Sajdah, merely lifting the head from the ground
without sitting up before the second Sajdah will nullify the Salaah.
MEN
