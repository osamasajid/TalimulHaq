WUDHU AND GHUSL
WUDHU AND GHUSL IS ALLOWED
WITH THE FOLLOWING WATER
1.
Rain water.
2.
Well water.
3.
Spring, sea or river water.
4.
Water of melting snow or hail.
5.
Water of a big tank or pond.
Water left over after drinking by human beings, Halaal animals and Halaal birds (e.g.
. cows,
goats, pigeons) is TAAHIR (clean)
WUDHU AND GHUSL IS NOT ALLOWED WITH THE FOLLOWING WATER.
1.
All NAJIS or impure water.
2.
Water extracted from fruit and trees.
3.
Water that has changed its color, taste and smell and becomes thick because something was soaked in it.
4.
Small quantity of water in which something NAJIS has fallen, e.g.
. Urine, blood, stool or wine or some
animal had died after falling into it.
