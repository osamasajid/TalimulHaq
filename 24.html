THE NIYYAT (Intention)
•
It is SUNNAT to make the NIYYAT for WUDHU.
•
For Wudhu one should make the Niyyat at heart that he is performing Wudhu to get rid of HADASE
ASGAR.
•
HADASE ASGHAR: Means the need of Wudhu.

DU'A BEFORE COMMENCING
WUDHU (Ablution)
I begin with the name of Allah
And all praises are for Allah.
1.
To wash one's hands, face and feet, etc. before performing SALAAH is called WUDHU or Ablution. No
Salaah is accepted without Wudhu.
2.
One should sit on a high, and clean place to perform Wudhu. Face the direction towards the Holy KA'BA
Sharif if possible.
3.
Using TAHIR (clean) water FIRST wash BOTH the HANDS up to the WRISTS THREE times.
