7.
Hazrat JIBRAEEL (Alayhis-Salaam) brought Allah's Book, Orders and messages to all the Prophets. He
was also sent to help the Prophets at times and fight against their enemies.
8.
Hazrat MICHAAEEL (A.S) is in charge of food and rain. Other Angels work under him who is in charge
of clouds, the seas, the rivers and the winds. He gets the order from Allah. He then order the others
under
his command.
9.
Hazrat IZRAAEEL (AS) takes away life with the order of Allah. He is in charge of death. Numerous
Angels work under him. Some take away the lives of good persons whilst others looking very fearful take
away the lives of sinners and disbelievers.
10.
Hazrat ISRAAFEEL (AS) will blow the SOOR ( TRUMPET) on the DAY OF JUDGEMENT. The sound
will destroy and kill everything that is on earth and in the skies. When he will blow for the second time all
will come to life with the order of Allah.
11.

enters all his BAD DEEDS, They are known as
KIRAAMAN-KAATIBEEN.
