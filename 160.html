12. TWO RAK'AATS NAFL SALAAH :
•
Perform two Rak'aats Nafl Salaah on the boundary of the Mataaf (the area where Tawaaf is performed). If
these two Rak'aats are omitted, there will be no harm. After completing the Salaah, or the seven Shawts, it
becomes Waajib to shave or trim the hair of the head, (if one wishes to release his Ihraam).
