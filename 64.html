Rabbanaa aatinaa fid-dunyaa hasanatan wa feel aakhirati hasanatan wa qinaa
'adhaaban naar
Our Lord! Give us in this world that which is good and in the Hereafter that which is good, and save us from the torment
of the Fire!
One who does not know the Dua e Qunoot may recite
or say thrice
or three times
Dua-E-Qunoot
O Allah : we beseech Your help.
and believe in You
and we praise You in the best
manner
and we ask Your pardon
and we put our trust in You
and we thank You and we are not ungrateful to You.
and we cast off, and leave one who disobeys You.
